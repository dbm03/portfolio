## About

My dad is a professional saxophone player based in Spain. He performs at weddings, corporate events, and private parties, bringing live music to liven up the occasion. He needed a website that could showcase his work, build trust with potential clients, and make it easy to get in touch.

I built this site for him as a single-page portfolio focused on conversion: show the music, show the reviews, make contact effortless.

## What it does

The site is structured as a series of full-width sections that guide visitors from first impression to contact:

- **Video hero**: A full-screen background video of a live performance with a prominent "Contact Me" CTA.
- **Photo & video gallery**: A CSS Grid layout with responsive image sizing, a lightbox modal for full-size viewing, and embedded YouTube videos of performances.
- **Client reviews**: Testimonial cards from past event clients with star ratings and expandable text, animated into view on scroll using Intersection Observer.
- **Bio**: Professional background, training, and experience.
- **Contact**: Phone number, WhatsApp link, and social media profiles (Instagram, YouTube, Facebook) for direct booking.

## How I built it

The site is built with **Next.js 12** (Pages Router) and **Tailwind CSS**. The entire page is composed from section components (`Hero`, `Gallery`, `Reviews`, `Bio`, `Contact`) rendered in sequence on the index page.

A few details worth noting:

- The mobile navigation uses a custom `useDelayUnmount` hook that keeps the drawer in the DOM for 500ms after closing, allowing the slide-out animation to complete before unmounting.
- Gallery images and review cards use `react-intersection-observer` to trigger fade-in and scale animations only when scrolled into view.
- The site is fully in Spanish, matching the target audience.
